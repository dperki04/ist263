Welcome to the Outdoors Guide!

Discover the joys of outdoors sports.

Our mission: To connect people with the outdoors through a collaborative effort between users. We are aiming to provide tips, tricks, reliable information, and guidance for responsible wildlife use to preserve our land for future generations.

Remember to always follow proper etiquette when interacting with others in the outdoors. Always use your best judgement and prioritize protecting yourself.


ALWAYS ensure you have the proper licenses and permits before engaging in public activities. Failure to comply can result in fines, jail time, or other legal repercussions. Our Activities page has recommendations, but always refer to MassWildLide.gov for updated laws.

⚠ Bird Watching Caution! Bird watching is open and popular all year round on public land. HOWEVER it is strongly recommended that bird watchers wear Blaze Orange for saftey, especially during hunting seasons, especially camoflaged seasons such as Turkey.

Safety & Etiquette

Always use your best judgement and prioritize protecting yourself.

  • Plan Ahead and Prepare
  • Travel and Camp on Durable Surfaces
  • Dispose of Waste Properly
  • Leave What You Find
  • Minimize Campfire Impact
  • Respect Wildlife
  • Be Considerate of Others
